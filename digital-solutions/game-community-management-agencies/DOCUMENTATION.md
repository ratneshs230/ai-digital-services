# Sentiment Analysis & Anomaly Detector

## Industry: Game community management agencies

### Overview
AI-powered tool that analyzes community sentiment in real-time and flags unusual activity, toxicity spikes, or emerging negative trends.

### Problem It Solves
Manual sentiment analysis is slow and subjective; detecting threats or PR crises early is difficult.

### Core Solution
NLP models continuously scan chat logs, forums, and social media. Anomaly detection identifies statistically significant shifts in sentiment or activity.

### Target Users
Community managers, PR teams, game developers.

### Business Impact
Proactive crisis management, improved community relations, data-driven content strategy, and resource allocation.

### Example Use Case
Sudden surge of negative feedback after a patch; identification of a coordinated harassment campaign.
