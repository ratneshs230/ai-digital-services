# AI-Powered Loan Underwriting Assistant

## Industry: Credit Unions

### Overview
Automates and accelerates loan application reviews using AI to assess risk and predict repayment likelihood.

### Problem It Solves
Manual loan underwriting is slow, costly, and prone to human error, leading to inconsistent decisions and potential losses.

### Core Solution
Machine learning models analyze applicant data (credit history, income, employment, etc.) to generate a risk score and recommend approval/denial, including suggested loan terms.

### Target Users
Loan officers, underwriters, credit risk managers.

### Business Impact
Reduces underwriting time, improves loan portfolio quality, minimizes defaults, and increases loan volume.

### Example Use Case
A loan officer uploads a loan application; the AI instantly provides a risk assessment and suggests optimal interest rate and loan duration.
